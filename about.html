Mental Peace

Description

Mental peace refers to a state of inner calm and tranquility, free from excessive stress, anxiety, or emotional turmoil.

It involves a balanced and harmonious state of mind where one feels content, focused, and in control of their thoughts and emotions. Achieving mental peace often requires practices such as mindfulness, meditation, healthy lifestyle choices, and positive thinking.

It can also be supported by strong social connections, engaging in enjoyable activities, and maintaining a good work-life balance.
